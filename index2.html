Fruits

Fruits are an essential part of a healthy diet, providing a wide range of vitamins, minerals, fiber, and antioxidants.

Vegetables

Vegetables are a vital part of a healthy diet, providing essential nutrients like vitamins, minerals, fiber, and antioxidants. They are low in calories and rich in nutrients that support overall health, including immune function, digestion, and heart health.

Dairy alternatives

Dairy alternatives, such as almond, soy, and oat milk, provide plant-based options for those avoiding dairy. They are often fortified with calcium and vitamin D, offering similar nutrients to dairy products while being suitable for lactose intolerant or vegan diets.
